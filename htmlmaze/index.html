Archive note

The server is gone.
The maze remains.

I built this in July 2025 as an experiment in shared, anonymous HTML: visitors wandered through generated rooms, wrote messages, and drew over one another’s walls.

On 20 July 2026, I retired the Rails server and sealed the maze. Everything here is static now. You can still explore and draw, but your marks disappear when you leave the room.
